About

ASBT Kalotsavam A Symphony of Talent

Dive into the spirit of ASBT Kalotsavam, our annual Arts Day where imagination meets expression. Experience a colorful celebration of music, dance, literature, and more — a festival where every art form finds its stage.

More than just a competition, ASBT Kalotsavam is a celebration of expression, passion, and unity. It is a platform where imagination is honored, voices are heard, and every performance adds color to the canvas of our student life. With events spanning dance, music, theatre, creative writing, visual arts, and more, ASBT Kalotsavam nurtures the soul of artistry within the scientific heart of our institution.

Join us as we celebrate the boundless world of art — where every note, step, stroke, and word tells a story.
