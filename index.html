General Rules and Regulations – Amrita Kalolsavam 2025

  1. Participation is open exclusively to students of Amrita School of Biotechnology.
  2. All participants must register through the official Kalolsavam registration website using accurate personal and academic details.
  3. A participant can register up to five (5) onstage and five (5) offstage events. There is no limit on the number of group events a participant can join.
  4. A participant can register as the team leader for only one group event. To participate in multiple group events with the same team, other group members must take turns registering as team leader using their own Roll Number.
  5. All possible events will be initially listed on the registration portal. Events that do not meet the minimum participation requirement will be cancelled, and the concerned participants will be informed accordingly.
  6. Once a participant registers for an event, withdrawals must be communicated to any Creatome Core Committee member within one (1) day of registration. Failure to do so will make the participant ineligible for further participation in Amrita Kalolsavam 2025.
  7. All participants are expected to maintain discipline, sportsmanship, and respectful conduct at all times. Misbehavior or violation of rules will result in disqualification.
  8. Event-specific rules can be accessed on the website by clicking the 'Rule Book' and must be followed strictly. Judges’ and coordinators’ decisions will be final and binding.
  9. Participants must be punctual and report to venues on time. Late arrivals may not be allowed to participate.

About

ASBT Kalotsavam A Symphony of Talent

Dive into the spirit of ASBT Kalotsavam, our annual Arts Day where imagination meets expression. Experience a colorful celebration of music, dance, literature, and more — a festival where every art form finds its stage.

More than just a competition, ASBT Kalotsavam is a celebration of expression, passion, and unity. It is a platform where imagination is honored, voices are heard, and every performance adds color to the canvas of our student life. With events spanning dance, music, theatre, creative writing, visual arts, and more, ASBT Kalotsavam nurtures the soul of artistry within the scientific heart of our institution.

Join us as we celebrate the boundless world of art — where every note, step, stroke, and word tells a story.
